Welcome to Muhle's Safe Haven, a sanctuary created with love and strength for survivors of Gender-Based Violence (GBV). Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and empowering environment where survivors can begin their journey towards healing and reclaiming their lives.

We believe that everyone deserves to live free from fear and harm, and we're here to walk with you every step of the way.
