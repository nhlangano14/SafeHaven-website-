Welcome to services

We offer a range of services: Emergency Shelter & Safe Housing A secure and confidential location where survivors can find immediate refuge. Counseling & Psychological Support Trauma-informed counseling by qualified professionals, both one-on-one and group therapy. Legal Aid & Advocacy Assistance with legal documentation, court support, and navigating the justice system. Medical Support & Referrals Access to medical care and guidance through our partner healthcare professionals. Skills Development & Empowerment Workshops Helping survivors build confidence and independence through education and training.

Muhle's Safe Haven is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering survivors of Gender-Based Violence (GBV). Together, we create a safe space filled with hope, healing, and restoration.
